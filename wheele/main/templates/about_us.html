About Us

Welcome to Wheel-E,where innovation meets sustainable transportation. We are a trailblazing electric taxi service committed to revolutionizing the way you travel, while also reducing our carbon footprint and contributing to a cleaner environment.

Our Mission

At Wheel-E, our mission is to provide a convenient, reliable, and eco-friendly mode of transportation that doesn't compromise on quality or comfort. We aim to lead the transition towards emission-free urban mobility by offering state-of-the-art electric vehicles for taxi services.

We value your privacy and security. We will never share your personal information with third parties without your consent. We also take your safety very seriously. All of our drivers are background checked and insured.

  • Convenient and reliable service
